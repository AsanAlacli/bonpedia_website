Upcoming Features

Stay up-to-date
with offers

Our daily alerts will allow you to keep you up-to-date with where to get your most frequent items for less money.

Nutri Score for Healthier Lifestyle

Keep track of what fills your shopping basket. Bonpedia allows you to keep track of your habit of spending on fast food vs. fruits and vegetables and help to reduce fast food consumption.

Shopping List Recommendation

Based on your previous shopping lists Bonpedia will
create for you a shopping list according to several
supermarkets' price list. You can then select the
supermarket that is the most cheapest for you.
This will allow you to save money!

Nutri Score

For each item our App will provide a nutri score: A to E with A meaning that the food item is healthy and E meaning that is rather not healthy. In addition, nutrition details about the product will be shown.

Daily Recipe Suggestions

We will allow the users to access daily recipe suggestions. Along with the recipe details, users will also have the information where to buy the ingredients most cheapest.

Menu Suggestions

Users can access menu suggestions for special events. The App will also show the information where to shop cheap.
